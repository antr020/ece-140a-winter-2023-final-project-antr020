WEEKLY PROGRESS

WEEK 6, Feb 20, 2023

During this week, our team members start build up ideas and construct a product's profolio

PRODUCT: TechWear Jacket
  • When you put your phone in your pocket it automatically starts charging,
  • Button for light or reflective if walking at night
  • Will automatic wireless charge via hanger
  • Potentially tap purchase like apple pay

Realizable: With current technology yes it is doable, small chips can fit inside of hoodies/jackets/sweaters

Attractive: Techwear is a subgenre in fashion which usually does not have actual technology, currently Arcteryx jackets are on top of the market due to their tactical look and waterproof material, and adding an aspect of technology would be useful

Testable: Add a no-wire charger into a sweater and have it charge.

SIMPLE Test

Simple: Yes it is simple, you put on the jacket put the phone in the pocket and it starts charging, to pay u just extend your arm and turn arm or press button

Improves: In the future we could have other products (types of jackets) according to buyers activities(like hiking, skiing, etc.) or working environment, or just casual.

Meaningful use case: convenient for common users, and safety for the outdoors versions

Proposition is justifiable: Clothing article with IoT

Lifestyle integration is easy: Yes you just simply put it on, and hang it on the hanger

Encourages sharing: Convenient charging is important since everyone uses a phone daily

RIBS Test

Relevant: Yes, it is relevant as it provides added convenience to a clothing item that people already wear

Inevitable: Techwear being applied to more parts of our clothing is inevitable

Believable: Yes, since the technology to build the jacket is available

Simple: It's simple to use, having a charger always on you when you wear the clothing item.

Target Market

Demographic: Tech people willing to explore, fashion person looking for the new thing

Geographic: There would be different thicknesses depending on the type of sweater so it is enjoyed in all regions

Psychographic: Both utilitarian and fashionable groups/ similar to current Arcteryx consumers

Job-To-Be-Done

Conveniently brings technology to you without having to carry or worry extra.

Competitors: Arcteryx, small solar panels/charger(attaches to backpack, wrist, etc), Amazon muti-function jackets

Unique value proposition: Techwear that you can actually just put it on

Revenue model: Business to consumer
